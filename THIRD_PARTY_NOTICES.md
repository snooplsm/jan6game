## Lamp post light

This project includes a converted and modified derivative of
["Lamp post light"](https://sketchfab.com/3d-models/lamp-post-light-ff5e3bc99dd146d986cb662813ec988e)
by [Sousinho](https://sketchfab.com/sousinho), licensed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/).

Project modifications include conversion from glTF Y-up coordinates to Unreal
Z-up centimeters, material reconstruction for Unreal, generated mesh LODs, and
simple convex collision. The original attribution text is preserved at
`SourceAssets/LampPostLight/LICENSE.txt`.
